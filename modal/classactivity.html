Four Different Shoes

Visvim Scagways

Visvim is a Japanese menswear brand founded by Hiroki Nakamura in 2000. The brand is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, and has stores in Japan and the US, and is sold internationally at luxury department stores and boutiques including Bergdorf Goodman in New York and Dover Street Market in London. At the heart of Visvim's philosophy is Nakamura's passion for heritage and craftsmanship.

Rick Owen Fur Shoes

Rick Owens is a high-end fashion brand founded by the American designer of the same name, Rick Owens. Established in 1994, the brand is renowned for its distinctive and edgy aesthetic, which blends elements of goth, punk, and minimalism. Rick Owens' designs are characterized by unconventional silhouettes, draping, and a preference for dark, monochromatic color palettes.With flagship stores in major fashion capitals worldwide, Rick Owens has established itself as a prominent player in the luxury fashion industry.

CCP Boots

CCP, short for "Carol Christian Poell," is a distinguished avant-garde fashion brand known for its uncompromising dedication to craftsmanship, innovation, and unconventional design. Founded by the Austrian designer Carol Christian Poell, the brand has earned a reputation for pushing the boundaries of traditional fashion norms.Established in the late 1990s, CCP has consistently challenged conventional fashion conventions by experimenting with materials, construction techniques, and silhouettes.

Margiela Tabis

Maison Margiela, often simply referred to as "Margiela," is a renowned Belgian fashion house known for its avant-garde and deconstructive approach to design. Established in 1988 by the enigmatic Belgian designer Martin Margiela, the brand quickly gained recognition for its unconventional and thought-provoking creations.Maison Margiela is celebrated for its distinctive style, characterized by deconstructed garments, oversized proportions, and a deliberate use of unconventional materials.
